a word about us


Tutor AI is a unique interactive learning web application for children of age group 4-8 years. Tutor AI provides an interactive platform making primary learning easy and fun for kids. Our platform will help children learn basic knowledge of alphabets, numbers, colours, shapes, and sizes. Pictorial charts and flowcharts will help kids learn quickly and grasp the topics. With the help of Tutor AI, children will learn about their environment and surroundings. The application comes with powerful AI tools which will help kids easily relate to real life.
